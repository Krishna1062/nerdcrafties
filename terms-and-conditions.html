ENROLMENT GUIDELINES AND POLICY

Last updated: July 1, 2022


➢For enrolment into your desired specialised course from NerdCrafties visit our website www.NerdCrafties.com
➢Read all the information and structures of the NerdCrafties premium courses.
➢Go to the course you are interested and fill the given google form with all the necessary details.
➢After completely filling the google form you are required to pay the respective fees for the specific course with the given link.
➢Once you complete your payment for the course then your admission will be confirmed.
➢In case you want to cancel your enrolment after paying the fees full refund will not be applicable.
➢20% amount will be deducted from your paid fees and remaining 80% amount will be credited in your account within 7 business days.
➢In case you want to cancel your enrolment in between once the course has been started the 40% amount will be deducted from fees.
➢After Successfully getting enrol into the program our administrators will inform you and will be in your touch for the further program schedules and structure.
➢All the lectures are going to be on live meeting platform for the purpose of great interacting and learning experience with faculty.
➢At the end of the program you will be getting ‘Certificate of Completion’ from NerdCrafties.
➢75% attendance is compulsory for the certification.
➢Keep Your attendance record and overall performance good to get Five stars on your certification.
➢If you Don’t follow the attendance criteria you will not be getting certification from NerdCrafties.
